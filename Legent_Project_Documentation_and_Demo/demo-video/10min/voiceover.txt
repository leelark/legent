Legent opens with a public product story for lifecycle email teams. The website explains why audience quality, creative governance, launch control, delivery safety, and revenue feedback need one operating layer.

The operator creates a workspace, signs in, and moves through onboarding. Workspace, sender, and provider readiness become explicit before the first campaign reaches production.

Inside the app, Email Studio and Audience Studio provide the daily operating surfaces. Teams manage templates, assets, subscribers, lists, segments, imports, preferences, consent, and suppression state.

Campaign Studio turns content and audience into a governed send. Operators configure audience rules, frequency caps, budgets, experiments, approval posture, and run a readiness scan before launch.

Delivery and tracking close the loop. Provider health, routing, queue stats, replay safety, open events, click events, experiments, budget ledger, and dead letter handling stay visible.

Automation, analytics, deliverability, settings, and admin complete the system. Journeys can be simulated and governed while admins control runtime policy, users, roles, branding, domains, and platform settings.

The product can continue into deeper enterprise controls, richer observability, stronger deliverability, advanced automation, and AI-assisted operating recommendations.
