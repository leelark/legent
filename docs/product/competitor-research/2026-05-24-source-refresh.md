## Current Source Facts

- Salesforce Marketing Cloud Engagement source refresh confirms separate depth across Content Builder/Email Studio, Contact Builder/Data Extensions, Journey Builder, Automation Studio, deliverability controls, Einstein AI, roles, audit, and SSO. Several features depend on edition, business unit, account settings, add-ons, contract terms, or enabled setup.
- Salesforce Automation Studio still frames automations around schedules, file triggers, imports, queries, file transfers, extracts, scripts, validation, and send activities. This keeps Legent's implemented SQL/import/file/extract/webhook/send slices partial until script sandboxing, target replay, target storage, and target delivery evidence exist.
- Salesforce Contact Builder sources confirm data extensions, relationships, retention, shared Data 360 segment activations, and high-volume deletion workflows. Legent's local relationship/cardinality and audit work remains a local contract until sendable-key migration, indexed relationship execution, and target retention evidence exist.
- Salesforce Einstein sources confirm generative content, send-time optimization, engagement frequency/scoring, and data-quality prerequisites. Legent's deterministic governance and approved schedule evidence is not equivalent to model-backed, recipient-level optimization.
- Adobe Journey Optimizer sources emphasize AEP-backed scheduled campaigns, real-time event-triggered journeys, channel configuration, email designer/templates, personalization expressions, AI assistant/agents, decisioning, content and path experiments, consent policies, reporting, and Customer Journey Analytics integration.
- Adobe Campaign v8 remains relevant for batch workflow automation, file/data management, approvals, transactional messaging, built-in reports, and deliverability monitoring.
- Braze sources emphasize no-code cross-channel Canvas journeys, real-time data activation, segmentation, Canvas experimentation, post-launch edits, reporting/analytics, BrazeAI assistance/decisioning, and deliverability diagnostics that do not prove inbox placement.
- Klaviyo sources emphasize dynamic segments, list/segment/metric/date-triggered flows, re-entry settings, predictive analytics with data prerequisites, AI-assisted segment definition with user responsibility, flow/campaign analytics, and deliverability health indicators.
- HubSpot sources emphasize active/static segments, workflow actions/branches, Breeze AI features, AI access and data-sharing controls, DNS authentication, delivery analysis, marketing email performance, and workflow performance reporting.

## Legent Interpretation

- Product claims should say Legent has local contracts for several slices, not full Salesforce/Adobe/Braze/Klaviyo/HubSpot parity.
- The most important remaining local product gaps are model-backed AI provider/runtime controls, artifact browsing/search, broader Journey/Automation executable node depth, persisted segment governance/audit APIs, draft count previews, indexed relationship/event execution, policy audit history, and runtime evidence surfaces.
- The most important evidence-bound gaps remain provider capacity, sender reputation, production egress, image provenance, restore/admission/TLS, live monitoring handoff, Kafka/runtime replay, target object storage, target analytics scale, and high-volume load proof.

## Cautions

- Do not mix Salesforce Marketing Cloud Engagement, Growth, Advanced, Next, Data 360, and Einstein feature sets without checking the exact product and license.
- Official vendor pages can be product-marketing level; prefer help-center or documentation pages for backlog acceptance criteria.
- AI features require explicit data-use, opt-in, human review, provider disclosure, metering, and audit controls before Legent can make feature claims.
- Deliverability sources describe diagnostics, configuration, reputation, warming, and measurement. They do not guarantee inbox placement.
- Predictive and optimization features often require sufficient historical data. Low-data/new sender behavior must stay conservative.
- This refresh updates source freshness only. It does not provide target runtime evidence or production readiness evidence.
