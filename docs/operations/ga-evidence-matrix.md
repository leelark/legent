## Open Blocker

Production egress is not release-ready until network policy is backed by real dependency data. `infrastructure/kubernetes/overlays/production/network-policy.yml` defines a default-deny policy for ingress and egress, but no explicit egress policy is present. This can block pod-initiated in-cluster/service egress as well as managed/external dependencies. Before production promotion, obtain the exact service dependencies, provider CIDRs/ports, or confirm that the selected CNI supports reviewed FQDN egress policy for required SMTP/API email providers, DNS, object storage, observability, and any managed data services.

## Evidence Storage

Store target-environment transcripts or signed summaries under `docs/operations/` or `.codex/reports/` with timestamp, environment, release version, commands run, pass/fail result, and owner. Keep secrets, raw tokens, credentials, private keys, and sensitive customer data out of evidence artifacts.
