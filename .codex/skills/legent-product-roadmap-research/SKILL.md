# Legent Product Roadmap Research

1. Use `.codex/commands/research-pass.md`.
2. Use current primary or official sources for external facts.
3. Separate fact, inference, gap, and recommendation.
4. Update `docs/product/salesforce-parity-matrix.md` or competitor research notes.
5. Convert implementation gaps into `.codex/backlog/queue.json` items.
6. Do not make unsupported parity, GA, deliverability, or throughput claims.

Keep research summaries concise and dated.
